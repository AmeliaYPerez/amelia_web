About Me

I am passionate about data science and statistics, and have experience developing machine learning models to solve business problems. During my internship at MS2 Consulting Group, I contributed to cost savings in the aquaculture industry by implementing ML models that reduced shrimp pond expenses and increased production. My technical skills include Python, SQL, R and various libraries and frameworks for data analysis and machine learning. I also have soft skills such as teamwork, effective communication, and an innate ability to solve problems efficiently. As a WiDS Medellin Ambassador, I play an active role in promoting diversity in data science and supporting women in the field. I have also completed a number of certified courses on recognized platforms, demonstrating my commitment to continuous learning and improving my data science skills. My goal is to use my knowledge and experience to address complex challenges using data and statistical analysis to make informed decisions and create a positive impact in the world.
